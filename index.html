Evaluation Context & Urgency

Following the June 2024 merger of Amira and iStation, the combined organization faces critical platform decisions with the SalesLoft renewal approaching November 15th. The current state reveals severe fragmentation: SalesLoft shows only 4-8 of 60 licenses being used effectively, teams operate across multiple disconnected conversation intelligence tools (Chorus for CS, Sybil for some reps), and critical processes like pilot management live in Google Docs.

Rich's (CRO) Dual Mandate

Two requirements: (1) Eliminate overlapping conversation intelligence tools to create unified customer visibility, and (2) Achieve 30% AI usage across every role with "agentic measures" - autonomous capabilities that replace workflows, not just assist with tasks.

⏰ Decision Timeline

With SalesLoft's renewal on November 15th, Amira must select a platform by mid to end October to allow for implementation overlap. This evaluation compares how Outreach address the specific needs of a K-12 education technology company navigating post-merger consolidation.
